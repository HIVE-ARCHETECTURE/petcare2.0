About Us

We believe pets of all types should be treated fairly and should live a safe, healthy and happy life. Our range of products and services are created for that very purpose; We have 100% organic pet food, outstanding kennel services and world class grooming services designed to really pamper your pets.

 

Aperture Pet Care always will be a family owned and operated company. It was founded in 2006 by Cave Johnson. He had a great dream to bring unity to the world of pet care.

 

He was successful, and Aperture Pet Care was born. It has expanded over hundreds of stores across Russia, Asia and Europe. Recently, our first American branch has opened in Club Alpha, New York.

 

Over many years, since its beginnings in 2006, Aperture Pet Care has evolved to be the healthy, safe and fun brand it is today. We promise it will always be that way!

 

Our Aperture Pet Care team go above and beyond for customers, by being able to provide expert advice and good old fashioned customer service (including carrying your items to the car!)

 

But the thing that really separates us from all the other pet care stores in this world, is the fact that we truly care about your pets. We really do take special time, effort and energy into making sure your pet is healthy, happy and safe. We don't do this for money, we do this because it is the right thing to do and keeps the world revolving smoother for everyone.

 

Each store is operated by animal specialists who are trained and knowledgeable across all areas of pet care. Our team, national and local, understand that pets are family and deserve the same level of comfort and care - we also believe we're responsible for making this a possibility.

 

That's why no matter what your budget is, you can find the best products for your pet available at Aperture Pet Care!

 

Visit us in store or call us and find out for yourself!
